Project detail

SO101 Arm · Visual Language Action Deployment

Deploying and benchmarking visual language action (VLA) models on our SO101 arm to unlock reliable autonomous manipulation demos—from tic-tac-toe to pick-and-place—on Jetson Thor.

Goal

Run NVIDIA GR00T 1.5 + local VLM pipelines for single-task autonomy.

Hardware

SO101 6-DoF arm and Jetson Thor

Focus

VLA inference, closed-loop control, onboard training experiments.

SO101 Arm Repo GR00T VLA Reference

Mission

Teach a robotic arm to reason about tasks the way we do.

We are replicating NVIDIA's GR00T 1.5 recipe and pairing it with a lightweight local VLM to understand the tradeoffs in latency, tactile response, and reliability for simple single-task experiences. Our immediate milestone is to autonomously play tic-tac-toe while narrating moves.

  • ✔ SO101 follower/leader teleoperation
  • ✔ Teleop-to-dataset pipeline
  • … Gr00t 1.5 finetuning for tic-tac-toe
  • … Local VLM integration for task determination
  • … Release reproducible deployment guide for campus outreach
